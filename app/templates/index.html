The Individuals

Models & Evaluation

Three complementary ML approaches evaluated with multiple metrics and stress tests to understand model limitations and guide improvement.

NLP Approach

01

Feature Extraction

spaCy NER identifies person mentions across 2,849 documents. VADER sentiment analysis scores surrounding context. Co-occurrence with incriminating terms (trafficking, minor, abuse) is computed per person.

02

Text Representation

TF-IDF vectorization with bigrams (max 500 features) creates document-level text features. Combined with 7 tabular NLP features for a hybrid feature space.

03

Classification

Binary task: predict if an individual faces consequences. Three models: Logistic Regression (baseline), Random Forest + TF-IDF (best), Legal-BERT (transformer).

04

Evaluation

Accuracy, F1 Macro, MCC, and precision/recall assessed. Feature ablation study and power tier stress test reveal model limitations and improvement paths.
